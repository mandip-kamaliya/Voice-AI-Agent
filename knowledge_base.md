# BrightBox Support Knowledge Base

## Document 1: Company & Product Overview

BrightBox is a monthly subscription box service delivering a curated mix of snacks and household essentials to customers' doors. Customers choose between three plans: the Starter Box (8–10 items, $19/month), the Family Box (15–18 items, $34/month), and the Custom Box (customer-selected items, priced per item).

Boxes ship on the 1st of every month. New subscribers who sign up after the 25th of the month will have their first box shipped on the 1st of the following month. Subscriptions can be paused, skipped for a single month, or cancelled at any time from the account dashboard, with no cancellation fee.

BrightBox currently ships within the United States and Canada only. International shipping is not yet available.

Customers can swap up to 3 items in their box each month before the monthly order-lock date (the 25th of the prior month). After the order-lock date, the box contents are finalized and cannot be changed.

## Document 2: Shipping, Returns & Billing FAQ

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US, and 5–8 business days for Canadian addresses.

Q: What do I do if my box arrives damaged?
A: Contact support within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the damaged item, and we'll send a free replacement or issue a credit to your account — your choice.

Q: Can I get a refund instead of a replacement?
A: Yes. Refunds are available for damaged, missing, or incorrect items within 14 days of delivery. Refunds for subscription cancellations are not provided for the current month's box once it has shipped, but future billing will stop immediately.

Q: When does my card get charged?
A: Your card is charged on the 25th of each month, ahead of the next box shipping on the 1st.

Q: Can I change my plan?
A: Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between Starter, Family, and Custom plans anytime from your account dashboard. Changes made before the 25th apply to the next box; changes made after apply to the box after that.

Q: Do you offer gift subscriptions?
A: Yes. Gift subscriptions can be purchased for 1, 3, 6, or 12 months, and the recipient receives an email notification with their first box shipment.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime from the account dashboard under Subscription Settings. There is no cancellation fee, and you'll continue to receive any box that has already shipped.

## Document 3: Support Escalation Policy

This document defines when the support agent should hand off to a human agent instead of answering directly.

Answer directly when the question is about:
- Shipping timelines, plan pricing, or box contents
- Standard return, refund, or replacement policy
- Billing dates or how to change a subscription plan

Do NOT answer directly, and instead offer to connect the customer to a human agent, when the question involves:
- A specific order number, account, or personal billing dispute the agent doesn't have access to look up
- A request that falls outside published policy (e.g. a customer asking for an exception)
- Anything involving anger, frustration, or a complaint that policy alone won't resolve

Example fallback line: "I don't have access to your specific order/account details, but I can connect you with a human agent who can help you with that."
